Help: View Profile

If you're unsure what to do on the view your profile page on the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: You are viewing your profile information.
  • Step 2: If you want to change your password click on "change password" button.
  • Step 3: If you want to delete your delete click on "Delete account" button.
  • Step 4: If you want to update your account click on 'Update Profile' button.
