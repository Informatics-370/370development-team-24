Help: Manage Booking Listing

If you're unsure what to do on the Manage booking page on the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: You are viewing the booking request information.
  • Step 2: If you want to accept an entertainer for the booking click on "Accept" button.
  • Step 3: If you want to decline an entertainer for the booking click on "Decline" button.
  • Step 4: If you want to search for a booking, enter the information in the search textbox.
