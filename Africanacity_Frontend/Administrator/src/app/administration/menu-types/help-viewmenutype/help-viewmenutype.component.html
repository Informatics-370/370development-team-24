Help: Actions to perform on the "View Menu Type Screen"

If you're unsure, about what to do on this Screen, please read thorugh the instructions below.

  • Action 1: Click on the "Add"
    • Sub-step 1:You will be re-directed to the "Add Menu Type"

  • Action 2: Click on the "Edit Icon"
    • Sub-step 1:You will be re-directed to the "Edit Screen"

  • Action 3: Click on the "Delete Icon"
    • Sub-step 1:A notification will pop-up, asking you to confirm your request to delete

  • Action 4: You can also search for a specific Menu Type

    • *Note: You do not have to perform the above actions. You can simply view the list.
