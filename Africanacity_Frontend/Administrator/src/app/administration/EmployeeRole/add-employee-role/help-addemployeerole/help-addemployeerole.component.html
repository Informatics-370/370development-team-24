Help: Adding an Employee Role

If you're unsure how to add an Employee Role to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in the Name of the Employee Role.
  • Step 2:Enter in a short description of the employee role.
  • Step 3: Add the employee role, by clicking the "Add" button
  • Step 4: Cancel the operation, by clicking the "Cancel" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the employee role
