Help: Adding an Employee

If you're unsure how to add an Employee to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in the Surname of the Employee.
  • Step 2:Enter in the First Name of the Employee.
  • Step 3:Select the"Gender"of the employee from the drop-down.
  • Step 4:Select the"Employee Role"of the employee from the drop-down.
  • Step 5:Enter in the Email Address the Employee.
  • Step 6:Enter in the Physical Address the Employee.
  • Step 7:Enter in the Phone Number the Employee.
  • Step 8: Add the item, by clicking the "Add" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
