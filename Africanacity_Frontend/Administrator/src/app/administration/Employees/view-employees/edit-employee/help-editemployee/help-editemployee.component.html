Help: Edit an Employee

If you're unsure how to edit an employee's information to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1:Edit the Surname of the Employee.
  • Step 2:Edit the First Name of the Employee.
  • Step 3:Edit the selected"Gender"of the employee from the drop-down.
  • Step 4:Edit the selected"Employee Role"of the employee from the drop-down.
  • Step 5:Edit the Email Address the Employee.
  • Step 6:Edit the Physical Address the Employee.
  • Step 7:Edit the Phone Number the Employee.
  • Step 8: Update the employee's information, by clicking the "Confirm" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
