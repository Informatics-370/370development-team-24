Help: Adding a schedule slot

If you're unsure how to add a schedule slot to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in the title of the Schedule slot.
  • Step 2:Enter in the date the Schedule slot.
  • Step 3:Input the start time of the Schedule slot.
  • Step 4:Input the end time of the Schedule slot.
  • Step 5:Select the"Event name"of the schedule slot from the drop-down.
  • Step 6:Select the"Schedule status"of the schedule slot from the drop-down.
  • Step 7:Provide a short description of the schedule slot.
  • Step 8: Add the slot, by clicking the "Add" button
  • Step 9:Cancel the action, by clicking the "Discard" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the slot
