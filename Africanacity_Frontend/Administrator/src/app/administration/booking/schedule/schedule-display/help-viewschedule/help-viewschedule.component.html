Help: View the Schedule Calendar

If you're unsure what actions to perform when viewing the schedule information, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1:Click on your desired view button from the options on the right: Month,week,day
    • Sub-step 1:Screen is adjusted to suit your view

  • Step 2:Edit the selected"Supplier Type"of the supplier type from the drop-down.
  • Step 3:Click on any event displayed on the calendar.
    • Sub-step 1:Event Details Modal will pop-up with more information about the event slot

    • Sub-step 3:Click the Edit button, and the "Edit Schedule Slot Screen" will be displayed

    • Sub-step 4:Click the Delete Button, and confirmation will be requested before deletion

    *Note: You do not have to perform the above actions. You can simply view the list.
