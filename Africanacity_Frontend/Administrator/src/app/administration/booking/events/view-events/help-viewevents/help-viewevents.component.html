Help: Actions to perform on the "View Entertainment Events Screen"

If you're unsure, about what to do on this Screen, please read through the instructions below.

  • Action 1: Click on the "Add Icon"
    • Sub-step 1:You will be re-directed to the "New Entertainment Event Screen"

  • Action 2: Click on the "Edit Icon"
    • Sub-step 1:You will be re-directed to the "Update Screen"

  • Action 3: Click on the "Delete Icon"
    • Sub-step 1:A notification will pop-up, asking you to confirm your request to delete

  • Action 4: You can also search for a specific Event by clicking on the search bar

    • *Note: You do not have to perform the above actions. You can simply view the event list.
