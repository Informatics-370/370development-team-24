Help: Adding an Supplier

If you're unsure how to add an Supplier to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in the Name of the Supplier.
  • Step 2:Select the"Supplier type"of the supplier from the drop-down.
  • Step 3:Enter in the Email Address the Supplier.
  • Step 4:Enter in the Physical Address the Supplier.
  • Step 5:Enter in the Phone Number the Supplier.
  • Step 6: Add the item, by clicking the "Add" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
