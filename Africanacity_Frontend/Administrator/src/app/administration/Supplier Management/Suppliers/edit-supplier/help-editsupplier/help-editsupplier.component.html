Help: Edit a Supplier

If you're unsure how to edit a Supplier's information to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1:Edit the name of the Supplier.
  • Step 2:Edit the selected"Supplier Type"of the supplier type from the drop-down.
  • Step 3:Edit the Email Address the Supplier.
  • Step 4:Edit the Physical Address the Supplier.
  • Step 5:Edit the Phone Number the Supplier.
  • Step 6: Update the supplier's information, by clicking the "Confirm" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
