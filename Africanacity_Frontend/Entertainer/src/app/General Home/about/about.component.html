OUR COMPANY

MISSION

Our mission is to bring entertainers together and a platform for upcoming artist to grow.

HISTORY

MMINO Restaurant was inspired by an event that Mr Mabaso had been doing called MMINO Sunday's, for the last five years. This event offered a live platform for artists of any kind, to come and showcase their talents. Artists were given an opportunity to showcase these talents to the locals and try and make a name for themselves. However, due to the constant hopping from venue to venue, Mr Mabaso decided that he needed a concrete place that would be seen as a suitable home for the various activities. MMINO Restaurant focuses on selling African cuisine, both modern and traditional foods.




MMINO Restaurant resides in Hatfield, Pretoria currently and focuses on offering African cuisine, as well as providing live entertainment to the guests.
