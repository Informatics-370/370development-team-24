MMINO Restaurant serves as a place for the people to be served both modern and traditional African cuisine. It is also seen as a safe place, given to various up and coming artists to showcase their unique talents, which gives these artists a platform to build and expand their brand, as well as to offer a form of live entertainment to people who are simply there to enjoy the food served. This organisation aims to merge both entertainment and hospitality.


MMINO is basically a conglomerate, and under that conglomerate, is where the various sectors lie. Some of these sectors include:
  • MMINO Outreach
  • MMINO Arts and crafts
  • MMINO Restaurant
