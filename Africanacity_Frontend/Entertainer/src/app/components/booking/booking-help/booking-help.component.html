Help: Make Booking

If you're unsure how to add an booking to the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in your name and surname.
  • Step 2: Enter in your email address.
  • Step 3: Enter in your phone number.
  • Step 4: Enter in your Instagram handle.
  • Step 5:Select your entertainment Type is.
  • Step 6:Choose a video demo or image demo that you want to upload
  • Step 7: Add the booking, by clicking the "Submit" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
