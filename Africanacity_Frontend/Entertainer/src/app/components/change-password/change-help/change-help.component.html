Help: Change password

If you're unsure how to change your password on the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in your old password.
  • Step 2: Enter in your new password.
  • Step 3: Enter in your confirm password.
  • Step 4: Save the new password, by clicking the "Submit" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
